Key Projects

HCMdrugs

Database

A web-based database cataloging the drugs available for the treatment of Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy (HCM). Currently in use by over 500 clinicians and researchers.

Integrative Transcriptomics & Network Medicine in Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy (HCM)

RNA-Seq / Computational Drug Repurposing

Analyzed human myocardial RNA-seq data to identify dysregulated genes and pathways in HCM, proposing zinc-based compounds and selective immune modulators as potential therapeutics.

Iron Nanoparticles Mitigate Textile Wastewater Toxicity in Wheat

Phytotoxicity Assays / RNA-Seq

Developed chemically and biologically synthesized iron nanoparticles to detoxify textile wastewater. Biological FeNP treatment improved wheat growth and restored key stress-responsive transcriptomic pathways, demonstrating a sustainable approach for wastewater remediation in agriculture.
